Privacy Policy

Imagekit

ImageKit, a Google Spreadsheet add-on, stores your image data only in your Google Drive, ensuring no one else has access.

ImageKit does not use your data for any other purposes than for the functioning of image inserting and processing tool.

We do not share your data to third parties or to other users of our tools in any way.

Security procedures are in place to protect the confidentiality of your data. The data is encrypted in transit, and it is stored on encrypted disks on the servers.

Google Spreadsheet add-on ImageKit only use official Google Apps Script URL fetch service for accessing online data.

Google Spreadsheet add-on ImageKit helps user pick images from different sources and insert them into user's Google Spreadsheet, ImageKit doesn't copy, transfer and store any image to its own or any other 3rd party server.

With Google services, add-on ImageKit will require only neccesary OAuth scopes, which are reviewed and approved by Google, and our Google Spreadsheet add-on will be installed only when you accept usage of those OAuth scopes. You can revoke our tools right to access your data at any point from your Google account control panel or by uninstall our add-on.

You find below all OAuth scopes required to use add-on ImageKit and the usage of each scope in this add-on:

drive.file : to store user computer uploaded images, also let user pick images from his/her Google Drive.

script.container.ui : to show addon menu

script.external_request : to fetch user provided URL target web page in order to get image link from page; to post users' add-on interaction data including image inserting time, action success or action fail to analytics tool server.

spreadsheets : to write image related data into user's Spreadsheet.

urlshortener : to shorten the Google Drive file long URLs for better data reading.

userinfo.email/userinfo.profile : to get unique user identity to associate with user's add-on interaction behavior including image inserting time, action success or action fail, in order to contact and assist user in case add-on fails to work.
